How to play

  • Teams: red vs blue, but everyone has a unique color — memorize your teammates here.
  • Score: bring the enemy flag to your base. Drop on death; teammates of the flag return it, enemies pick it up.
  • Roles: randomly assigned King, General, or Spy at game start. King kills General, General kills Spy, Spy kills King. Same role: first toucher wins.
  • Reveal: after a kill, only the killer and victim learn each other's roles. Nobody else.
